What is Telemedicine?

Telemedicine allows you to consult a doctor or healthcare professional remotely through video calls or online consultations. You can receive medical advice, prescriptions, and more without leaving your home.

How Telemedicine Works?

1. Scheduling an Appointment: Choose a time and select your doctor.

2. Prepare for the Call: Make sure you're in a quiet and well-lit place for the video call.

3. Consultation: Your doctor will join the video call at the scheduled time and provide medical advice based on your needs.

4. Follow-Up: If necessary, additional consultations can be scheduled or your doctor will provide instructions for care.

Common Uses of Telemedicine

1. Routine Checkups: Regular checkups for chronic conditions or preventive care.

2. Mental Health Consultations: Remote therapy and counseling sessions.

3. Prescription Services: Get prescriptions for medications or treatments without visiting the clinic.
