Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Everything you need to know about MediHub

What is MediHub?
MediHub is a comprehensive digital healthcare platform offering online appointment bookings, home visits from qualified medical teams, and personalized prescription services for patients after their consultation or treatment.
How do I book an appointment?
You can book an appointment by signing in to your account and selecting the type of service you need, whether it's a telemedicine consultation or a home visit. Choose your preferred doctor or medical team and available time slots.
Do I need to pay for the home visit service?
Yes, the home visit service is chargeable. The cost is based on your location and the type of medical team (doctor or nurse) requested. You will receive a detailed breakdown of the charges before confirming the appointment.
Can I change my appointment time?
Yes, you can reschedule your appointment up to 24 hours before the scheduled time. You can log in to your account and change the appointment details in the "My Appointments" section.
How are prescriptions handled?
After your consultation, your doctor will issue a prescription based on your diagnosis and treatment plan. The prescription can be sent directly to your email or to a pharmacy of your choice for fulfillment.
Is my medical information secure with MediHub EliteCare?
Absolutely. We prioritize your privacy and follow strict security measures to ensure your medical information is protected. All data is encrypted and stored securely to comply with healthcare privacy regulations.
